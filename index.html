Who we are

Welcome to the Nephilim, where we share our enthusiasm for video games and our memories. By coming here, you have found the place where you can live only to create and remember wonderful memories that go beyond gaming accomplishments or scores.

No matter what game we end up playing, our goal is to continue our gaming journey as a family. Above all, we take pride in our competitive mindset with the various collections of players we have gathered over the years who have different backgrounds and skillsets but all share the same magnanimous mindset to support one another before thinking of our own personal achievements.
